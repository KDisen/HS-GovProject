Education and Economy

Economy and education run hand in hand when it comes to Americas Prosperity. Lower class areas do not have much access to educational resources which influences why they are incapable of obtain efficient jobs to remain financially stable.

To do this we want to

  • Create institutions that are focused on careers people are passionate about.
  • Decrease money for the military and invest in schools
  • Develop better educational systems in lower class neighborhoods
