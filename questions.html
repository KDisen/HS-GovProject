Questions

DanielPerez: What are you going to do for undocumented immigrants?

LebronJames: Where will you get funding for these programs?

Innovation Party: We have seen that more than 50% of our spending is on military. We want to take a bit from the military expenses and invest in schools and education.

MsRivera: How are you going to enforce gender equality?

Innovation Party: We cannot enforce gender equality because everyone has different opinions, however, we will start by making salaries equal and banning questions related to gender/ race on job applications.
