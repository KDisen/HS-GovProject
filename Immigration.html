Immigration

Since the establishment of the United States, immigrants have been a major influence on Americas prosperity. It is devastating to address the fact that these people come here for a hand, but receive the boot. 121 family members were removed from the country by Immigration Customs Enforcement and 2.5 million people were deported.

How will we be better at this?

  • Create schools or programs that will help further their education.
  • Abolish the USCIS exam due to how unfair it is.
  • Create Labor laws to guarantee the safety of these immigrants who work for big businesses.
