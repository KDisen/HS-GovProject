Women's Suffrage

For centuries women have been degraded-- not receiving equal rights nor pay. Even after legal bills were passed to help enfranchisement of women (19th amendment). Society still faces these gender barriers. The Innovationists want to change this.

To do this we want to

  • Create programs that give women the same amount of work and training skills
  • Make Male - dominated careers more diverse by considering an equal amount of male and female applicants.
  • Make applications at random
  • eliminating questions regarding name and gender.
